About Me

I am an interdisciplinary engineer and data professional specializing in sustainable manufacturing, digital transformation, process optimization, and data-driven decision making. Holding an Erasmus Mundus META4.0 Master's degree from NTNU (Norway), Politecnico di Torino (Italy), and École Centrale de Lyon (France), I combine deep technical engineering knowledge with strong analytical and visualization skills (Power BI, Python, SQL, etc.).

My experience spans R&D, simulation (CFD, FEA), CAD/CAM, LCA/ESG analysis, and manufacturing systems across automotive, energy, and heavy industry sectors. Passionate about bridging engineering excellence with sustainability and intelligent technologies.
